Tip

Make sure to tag on with your OVV Chipkarte before heading down the stairs to the train! There is a second tag on point for the sprinter trains, but you will get fined €20 if you miss the first tag on point!

You can visit https://www.uitcheckgemist.nl/ to try and get your money back.
